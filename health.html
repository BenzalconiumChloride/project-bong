Best for Daily Support

Wysa

An AI-powered mental health chatbot that provides emotional support through conversations and therapeutic tools.

  • Evidence-based therapeutic techniques (CBT, DBT, mindfulness)
  • Mood tracking and journaling features
  • Tools for anxiety, depression, and stress management
